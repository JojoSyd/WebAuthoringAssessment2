The Manga

What is the manga about?

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is a long running action series, with each part focusing on a different main character, who are all part of the Joestar family tree in some form or another.

As the series progresses, forms of battling are introduced, initially through the power of hamon energy, concentrated power created through breathing, but goes on to become the power of Stands, ethereal forms that stand next to their user that have special abilities.

Stands were introduced as a way to keep things fresh and interesting, as it allows for more variety in terms of story telling and enemies.
